CHIN UPS

Let's Begin


Working of muscles while doing Chin ups

Reps- 5*2
Estimated Time- 2 min/set

The a chin-up is a variation of the pull-up exercise in which the reps are performed with the palms facing toward the body, in an underhand position, with a grip that is narrower than shoulder-width. Like other pull-up variations, it builds strength and muscle in the upper back, biceps, and core, but it utilizes the biceps slightly more than overhand pull-ups.
